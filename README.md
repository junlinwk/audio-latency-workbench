## 繁體中文

### 專案簡介
**SyncCircle** 是基於網頁的高精度音訊延遲測量工具。透過視覺化的圓環幫助使用者測量藍牙耳機、喇叭或音效卡的輸出延遲。

### 如何使用
1.  點擊 **開始測試** (或按 Enter)。
2.  聽取規律的節拍，並在聲音響起的瞬間按下 **空白鍵** 或 **點擊圓環**。
3.  建議連續點擊 10 次以上以獲得穩定的統計值。
4.  **校準建議**：先使用「有線耳機」測得基準值，再與「無線耳機」對比，兩者相減即為最精確的無線傳輸延遲。

## English

### Project Overview
**SyncCircle** is a high-precision, web-based audio latency measurement tool.  It utilizes a visual ring to help users estimate the output latency of Bluetooth headphones, speakers, or sound cards.

### How to Use
1.  Click **Start Test** (or press Enter).
2.  Listen to the steady beat and press **Space** or **Click the Ring** exactly on the beat.
3.  It is recommended to tap at least 10 times for a stable average.
4.  **Calibration Tip**: Test with wired headphones first to establish a baseline, then compare with your wireless device to isolate the transmission latency.
